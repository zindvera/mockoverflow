About MockOverflow

MockOverflow is a simple tool that helps you see how a sticker or a poster looks on different objects before you buy it. Sometimes it is hard to imagine if a design will look good on your specific items just by looking at a picture online.

What can you do?

You can use your phone's camera to put a digital sticker or poster on any flat surface around you. You can use your fingers to move the sticker, rotate it, and change its size. This helps you see the sticker from different angles and get a general idea of how it fits on your things.

You can try this on many different surfaces, such as:

✓ Laptop (Back or Palm rest)
✓ Tablets and iPads
✓ Phone cases
✓ Journal diaries & notebooks
✓ Refrigerator doors
✓ Washing machines
✓ Walls (for posters)
✓ Any other flat/plane surface

How to use it

  1. Find a sticker or poster you like on our website.
  2. Tap the "Open Camera" button.
  3. Point your camera at the surface where you want to put the sticker.
  4. Use one finger to drag and move the sticker.
  5. Use two fingers to pinch and rotate to change the size and angle.
  6. If you like how it looks, click the link to visit the seller's store and buy the real product.

Our goal is to give you a helpful preview so you feel more confident about your decorations. We show designs from our own collection and from independent sellers on sites like Etsy and Redbubble.
