Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount. Because MockOverflow uses your camera to preview stickers and posters, we maintain a strict "local-only" policy.

1. Camera Privacy

When using our visualization tool, our code requests access to your camera to overlay designs. This video feed remains strictly on your device. We do not record, store, or transmit any video or photo data of your home or workspace to our servers.

2. Use of Cookies

To provide a better user experience, we use a small data file called a "cookie" (or local browser storage). This is used solely to remember if you have already viewed our Tooltip Guide. This ensures you aren't interrupted by instructions once you already know how to use the tool.

3. No Personal Data Collection

MockOverflow is designed to be used anonymously. We do not collect or store your name, email address, or physical location during your browsing session.

4. External Marketplaces

Our "Get This Item" links take you to external sites like Redbubble or Etsy. We do not pass your camera data or browsing history to these stores. Once you leave MockOverflow, your activity is governed by the privacy policy of that specific marketplace.
